Dog's Spa

Unleash the Inner Pampered Pup

At PawPup, we believe your furry friend deserves a little luxury. That's why we offer a full-service dog spa within our cafe, allowing your pup to experience top-notch pampering while you enjoy a relaxing break.

More Than Just a Bath

Our experienced and caring dog groomers go beyond a simple wash. We offer a variety of spa services tailored to your dog's specific needs and breed. Here's what you can expect:

  • Deep Cleaning Baths: We use gentle, veterinarian-approved shampoos and conditioners to leave your dog's fur squeaky clean and smelling fresh.
  • Breed-Specific Grooming: Our groomers are familiar with different coat types and are skilled in providing haircuts and styles tailored to your dog's breed.
  • Nail Trimming & Paw Pad Care: We'll safely trim your dog's nails to prevent discomfort. We also provide paw pad treatments to keep them soft and healthy.

Safety & Comfort are Our Priority

  • Calm and comfortable environment designed for dogs.
  • Use of only gentle and pet-safe products.
  • Experienced groomers trained to handle all sizes & temperaments.

Swimming Pool

Making a Splash Together: About Our Unique Human & Dog Swimming Pool

At PawPup, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences for both humans and their canine companions. That's why we're proud to offer Lucknow's first dog cafe with a one-of-a-kind swimming pool designed for fun and refreshment for you and your furry friend!

A Pool Paradise for All:

Take a refreshing dip in our sparkling human pool, perfect for cooling off on a hot day.

Unleashing the Fun for Your Pup:

Your dog will love splashing around in the dedicated dog pool. Here's what makes it special:

  • Safe and Spacious: The dog pool is designed with a gentle entry ramp and a shallow depth, ideal for all dog sizes and swimming abilities.
  • Clean and Healthy: The dog pool is rigorously maintained with a separate filtration system, ensuring clean and healthy water for your furry friend.

The Perfect Bonding Experience:

Our human-dog swimming pool offers a unique opportunity to create lasting memories with your pup. Imagine the joy of splashing around together, fetching water toys, or simply enjoying the refreshing water on a hot day. It's the perfect way to cool down, exercise, and bond with your furry best friend.

Play Area

Unleash the Fun: All About Our Pawsome Play Area!

At PawPup, we understand the importance of playtime for our furry patrons. That's why we've created a dedicated dog play area that's a wonderland of fun and socialization!

A Paradise for Playful Pups:

  • Spacious and Safe: Our play area is designed to be both spacious and secure, allowing dogs of all sizes to run, jump, and play freely.
  • Variety of Playful Activities: We offer a wide selection of stimulating toys to keep your pup entertained. This includes:
    • Interactive Toys: Fetch toys, tug-of-war ropes, squeaky toys, to keep their minds and bodies active.
    • Agility Equipment: Tunnels, ramps, weave poles, and jumps to provide a fun challenge and encourage exercise.
    • Play Pools: A shallow pool for splashing fun on hot days.
    • Socialization Zone: A designated area for friendly pups to meet and play with new canine companions.

Supervised Fun for Peace of Mind:

  • Trained Staff: Our staff is trained to monitor playtime, ensuring safety and fair play for all dogs.
  • Clean & Hygienic: Our play area is regularly cleaned and disinfected to maintain a healthy environment for your pup.

More Than Just Fun:

  • Exercise & Stimulation: Playtime promotes physical activity and mental stimulation.
  • Socialization Benefits: Playing with other dogs helps your pup develop social skills.
  • Stress Relief: A good play session helps relieve boredom and stress.

Let the Fun Begin!

Bring your dog to PawPup and let them loose in our pawsome play area. We guarantee a tail-wagging good time for your pup and a relaxing break for you!
